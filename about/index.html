About

In no particular order: engineer, security researcher, tinkerer, devil’s advocate, husband, dad, cyclist, recovering cynic.

Broad range of professional interests which include technical recruiting, team building, and mentoring. Focused on building healthy, proactive engineering culture through empathy, shared responsibility, automation, and empowerment.

Engineering joy derived from designing and building: distributed platforms, service integrations, CI/CD pipelines, security pipelines, and automated tests of all types to name a few. Automation alleviates toil and increases feature velocity.

Security interests include technical and organization risk assessment, threat modelling, education and mentoring. Building a security program from the ground up is an enormous challenge, but one that is a passion to pursue.

Blog post topics explore interests and research hobbies, as well as provide a much needed opportunity to practice writing.
