Hacking the Interview: Algorithms and Big O

Here is a curated collection of resources for leveling up algorithms and complexity analysis.

  • Wikipedia – Time complexity – not beginner friendly, but a good place to start and a good reference document. Contains a comprehensive list of different notations with example algorithms.
  • Hacker Earth – Time and space complexity - contains detailed, helpful examples.
  • Free Code Camp – The 30 minute guide to rocking your next coding interview – long, detailed, and beginner friendly article. Contains its own list of curated, high quality resources.
  • Samson Hu – How to Pass Technical Interviews – mix of really high quality practical advice on technical interviews.
