Have I Been Pwned?

Check here now

I’ve had the same personal email address for a long time now. So it doesn’t come as much of a surprise that there were a list of results when searching for it on Have I Been Pwnd? The size of the list, at ten hacks, was a little surprising.

Equally surprising, my work email had been included in hacks as well. This, despite being registered with far fewer accounts and all with good reputations.

All of this emphasizes the need for good password hygiene, including using strong, unique passwords for every account.
