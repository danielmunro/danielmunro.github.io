About Me

In no particular order: engineer, security researcher, tinkerer, devil’s advocate, champion of underdogs everywhere, nerd, husband, dad, cyclist, recovering cynic.

Engineering

A broad range of technical interests, including building distributed systems, integrations, CI/CD pipelines, security tooling, automation, testing, incident response, and process optimization.

Leadership passions include strategic planning, technical recruiting, team building, leveling up colleagues, and knowledge sharing.

Focused on building healthy, proactive engineering culture through empathy, shared responsibility, autonomy, ownership, empowerment, and speed through quality.

Finds joy in designing and building: distributed platforms, service integrations, CI/CD pipelines, security pipelines, and automated tests of all types, to name a few.
